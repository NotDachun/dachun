I was also an inaugural entrepreneurship fellow at Madrona Venture Labs.

Previously, I was a Software Development Engineer Intern at Amazon, where I implemented a distributed index for Amazon's largest data lake; AI Research Fellow at Giving Tech Labs, where I constructed a social impact funding knowledge graph; and, a Teaching Assistant for CSE 446 Machine Learning at UW.
